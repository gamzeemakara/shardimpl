Donations
	[Adding more]
		If you wish to add more donation options, go to the DonationManager class (package com.rs.worldserver.sql)
		In the "startProcess" method, simply add an additional case, which corresponds to the productId found
		in the Paypal script provided in the website section.
		
Voting
	[Server sided]
		In the reward command class, modify the cases to match that of your vote rewards in your admincp
		
	[Website]
		This may be done through the RTL admin control panel
